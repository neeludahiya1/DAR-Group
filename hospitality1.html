Hospitality



DAR Hotel Pvt.Ltd

Thriving on its extensive knowledge of the global market and background in the service industry, DAR Group diversified its operations in the hospitality sector to set new standards in hotels across the country. DAR spectrum of Hotels brings to you luxury with a distinctive style for providing the best in service, standard & quality.

Combining tradition with modern amenities, every hotel project will be intelligently designed by a sense of harmony to bring unique experience to our guests. We will offer only the finest in hospitality and chromatic services tailored to meet the highest industry standards. Our world class facilities are well equipped for both entrepreneur and leisure travelers.

Once the group was assured that it had the additive to not only open hotels but also manage & compete with the best, they decided to move full steam ahead. It is after aligned determination that DAR comes up with the chain of hotels which would be domestic by nature but international by standards. DAR Group plans to expand its Hospitality business very rapidly.

Currently 3 ongoing projects are pride of the group: First is a 5-Star deluxe in Sector 32, Noida City Centre with 250 keys, following with the Greater Noida project in Sector 4 a 5-Star facility with 150 keys on 2.5 acres and now in Shirdi, a Pilgrimage Centre again a 5-Star with 150+ keys on 3 acres land. DAR has put in place a complete route map for its future expansion plan to drive on which it proudly expresses as The Seven notes of Hospitality Medley.
