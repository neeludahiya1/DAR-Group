Housing



DAR Housing Limited:

DAR Housing Limited is planning a number of Luxury Housing and Real-Estate projects at various locations in India.

On-going Project of DAR Housing:

Noida Extension Project : The ongoing residential project "DAR-ATS Rhapsody" in Noida Extension accommodates 1800sqft. & 2400sqft. luxurious residential flats. This project is having 7 multi storey towers. In 2400 square feet flats there are only 2 flats on each floor. It is amongst the best that one can imagine with all the units having a 360 degree view. This project is amongst the flourishing innovations in a residential property. In case of 1800 square feet flats there are 4 flats on each floor, making all the flats three side open and corner. It happens to be the foremost best-in-class luxurious and the most spacious projects of Noida Extension. Amenities like Central Park, Club House and Sporting facilities are included as per the international standards. Club house is having indoor games, party hall, gym and other facilities. Central area is landscaped in different theme gardens.

Upcoming Projects of DAR Housing:

Noida Expressway Project : Noida Expressway Project will be an exclusive luxury development on over 9 acres at sector-150. The project will be over 1 million of residential space in the posh locality of Noida Expressway with pleasant surroundings closely connected with all important places of Delhi NCR.

Yamuna Expressway Project : Yamuna Expressway Project will be a well-designed spacious apartment spread on 20 acres land. This project will be over 2 million residential spaces situated in close proximity to a number of everyday emergency locations that one can access in the shortest span of time.
