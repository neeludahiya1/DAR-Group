About Us

The year 1945 saw the humble beginning of a new era in entrepreneurship. Today after 72 years with interests spanning across a wide spectrum of business activities from Mica Mining, Real Estate to Exports the group is poised to launch itself into the next generation of expansion. It’s DAR GROUP, a name that symbolizes Innovation, Transparency and Trust.

A team of young and dynamic professionals is spearheading the Group to expand into diverse fields like Housing, Aviation, Hydroponics and Luxury Fashion. Apart from these a formidable range of Projects in the Hospitality with well-known National and International names has helped establish the Group in the future growth path. DAR is a name today that will keep inspiring new generations and fuel new imaginations.
